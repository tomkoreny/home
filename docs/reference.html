Layers (held with a thumb)

🧭 Nav · left thumb (Spc)

VI-style arrows on the right home row (H J K L = ← ↓ ↑ →), plus Home/End/PgUp/PgDn and the edit cluster (Undo/Cut/Copy/Paste/Redo).

🖱️ Mouse · left thumb (Tab)

Mouse movement, scroll and buttons on the right hand.

🎵 Media · left thumb (Esc)

Volume / mute, prev / play / next, brightness.

🔢 Num · right thumb (Bsp)

Number pad on the left hand (7 8 9 / 4 5 6 / 1 2 3, 0 on the thumb) + symbols on the outer column.

🔣 Sym · right thumb (Ent)

Shifted-number symbols ! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) on the left hand.

🔠 Fun · right thumb (Del)

F1–F12 on the left hand; media/print keys.

🔘 Button · hold Z or /

Clipboard / mouse-button helpers.

🇨🇿 CZ · tap V+M

Czech accents (the mauve letters). Tap V+M again to exit.

Layer-card contents follow standard Miryoku (your nav is the VI variant). The base letters, home-row mods and Czech accents are exact from your keymap; tell me if a layer differs.

🇨🇿 Czech diacritics

★ Type them directly — tap V+M, then the letter

A firmware CZ layer puts every Czech accent on its own letter key. Press the V+M chord to toggle it on, type the accented letters, press V+M again to exit. Everything else (space, mods, unaccented letters) keeps working normally.

KeyGivesKeyGives
a  e  i  o  u  yá é í ó ú* ýwě
c  s  z  r  t  d  nč š ž ř ť ď ňu  /   jů  /   ú

u → ů (the common one); ú is on j. ě is on w. All 15 letters covered: á č ď é ě í ň ó ř š ť ú ů ý ž.

Works in GTK/Electron apps (Firefox/Helium, mail, chat) via Linux Unicode mode. Capital accents and terminals/Qt aren't covered — use Super+D there.

★ Whole phrases / fix-ups — Super+D

Highlight text and press Super+D; the diacritics-fix script runs it through the claude CLI to add diacritics + fix typos, then types it back.
